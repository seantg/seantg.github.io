Ring Tradition

The Virginia Tech Ring Tradition traces back for over 100 years. Every year, the Class Ring is redesigned by students and tailored to the characteristics of their respective class. The ring is then premiered at Burruss Hall in the fall. In the spring, the Ring Dance is held. The Ring Dance symbolizes the transition from junior to senior. There are fireworks, the ring figure, and the sabre arch throughout, as well as multiple events through the week leading in.

The Leadership Team

During the spring of Freshman year, Class Officers are elected and learn of their duties. Thereafter, they work together to choose the members of the three committees that work with the Alumni Association to plan and execute Student Programs like the Ring Dance, the Ring Premiere and the Kickoff Concert. They spend countless hours to create the best experience that they can for their class, so thank one of them if you see them around campus!
